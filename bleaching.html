Bleaching

Brighten Your Smile with Professional Whitening

At our office, we offer two effective teeth whitening solutions: custom take-home bleaching trays and in-office whitening. Both methods deliver impressive results while being safe, personalized, and designed for your comfort and convenience.

Comparison

Take-Home Whitening vs. In-Office Whitening

OPTION 1

Take-Home Whitening

Custom take-home whitening is our recommended first step for most patients, especially those with busy schedules and minimal dental corrections needed.

How it works

  • We take molds of your teeth to create custom trays tailored to your mouth.
  • You’ll apply a small drop of bleaching gel into each tray section, targeting the front 10–12 teeth on both the upper and lower arches.
  • Wear the trays for 30 minutes per day for 10–14 days.

Perfect for patients who

  • Want a gradual and controlled whitening process
  • Are satisfied with their tooth alignment and shape
  • Prefer a more affordable solution

What to expect

  • 5–8 shade improvement in brightness
  • Results maintained with 30-minute touch-ups every 3–6 months
  • The bleaching gel lasts 1–2 years if stored in the refrigerator

OPTION 2

In-Office Whitening

As with the take-home option, we recommend maintenance bleaching every 3–6 months to preserve your bright smile. We’ll guide you every step of the way to ensure you achieve and maintain the whitening results you’re looking for.

How it works

  • Typically completed over two sessions in our office
  • Each session is equivalent to 4–5 take-home treatments
  • You’ll still receive custom trays for ongoing at-home maintenance

Perfect for patients who

  • Patients who want to whiten teeth quickly for an event
  • Those who prefer a more supervised process
  • Anyone who cannot commit to 10–14 days of at-home bleaching
